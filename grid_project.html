Reach Me Fast
chances : 3

Rules

  • You will find a grid and it has start and end positions.Your task is to reach the end point.
  • You can move in four directions(right,left,down,up).
  • The total number of chances is 3.
  • To pass the level you have to find shortest path.
  • The red color indicates the wall .If you move to the red grid chances will be reduced by 1
  • If you step into the wrong grid the chances will be reduced by 1.
